Decorating with plants is simple

To create the most interesting display use a mixture of plants sizes and leaf shapes. A large plant like Pippa the peace lily, mixed with something sculptural like Susie the snake pland and a soft fern like Bertie gives an attractive range of textures. Hanging plants like Rapunzel the golden pothos, add interest at different heights. Don#t forger yout pots. Matching pots will look clean and elegant, while a mix of styles and pattern look electric and fun.
